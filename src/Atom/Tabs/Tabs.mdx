# Tabs

The `Tabs` component lets users move between related views without leaving the
current workspace. It supports horizontal and vertical layouts, default or
controlled active keys, and accessible `tab` / `tabpanel` relationships.

<Primary />
<Controls />

## What To Validate

- The active tab and active panel stay in sync.
- `onChange` reports the selected tab key.
- Each tab is linked to its panel with `aria-controls` and `aria-labelledby`.
